Quaternius town asset subset
============================

The models in `medieval/` are derived from the free Standard edition of
Quaternius' Medieval Village MegaKit:
https://quaternius.com/packs/medievalvillagemegakit.html

The models in `nature/` are derived from the free Standard edition of
Quaternius' Stylized Nature MegaKit:
https://quaternius.com/packs/stylizednaturemegakit.html

Original models by Quaternius (@Quaternius).

License: CC0 1.0 Universal (CC0 1.0), Public Domain Dedication
https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/

For this project, the selected source glTF models and their PBR textures were
repacked as self-contained GLB files. Textures were resized to at most 512 px
and encoded as WebP. Geometry is not Draco- or Meshopt-compressed, so no
additional geometry decoder is required at runtime.
